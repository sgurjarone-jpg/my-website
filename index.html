About Me

Professional Photo

Singer & Performer

My singing journey began at a small stage in my hometown, singing devotional Bhajans for local gatherings. Over the years that passion grew into a full-time career — performing Bollywood hits, soulful Bhajans, and unforgettable live shows across weddings, festivals and corporate events.

Every performance is built on years of practice, genuine emotion and a deep love for music. Whether it's an intimate gathering or a stage of thousands, my goal is always the same — to make the audience feel every word.
